Sangathan Parva

25 Years of Sydney Satsang  •  4 – 12 July 2026

Jay Shree Swaminarayan 🙏

Join us as we celebrate 25 years of Sydney Satsang with a special week of bhakti, satsang, seva and sangathan. 🪺

Throughout the Week

  • 🛐 Satsangi Jeevan Parayan
  • 🎭 Cultural Programs
  • 👦🏾 Bal Mahotsav
  • 👏🏻 Seva, Satsang & Sangathan

With programs for children, youth and adults — there’s something for everyone.

Honoured Guests

🌟

We are blessed to have 1008 Acharya Shree Koshalendraprasadji Maharaj and santo from Shree Swaminarayan Mandir Bhuj present throughout the celebrations.

Don’t miss this rare opportunity to receive their blessings and enjoy their divine company.

We encourage you and your family to be part of this historic milestone. 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
