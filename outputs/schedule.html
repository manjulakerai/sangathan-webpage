Mandir Etiquette

  • Modest clothing — Please cover shoulders and knees when visiting the Mandir
  • Remove footwear — Shoes must be removed before entering the prayer hall
  • Head covering — Women are encouraged to cover their head during aarti and puja
  • Comfortable layers — Sydney in July can be cool; bring a warm layer

Mealtimes & Bhojan

  • Lunch — 12:30 PM – 2:00 PM

Dietary Notes

  • All meals are pure vegetarian (satvik)
  • No onion, no garlic
  • Jain options available on request
  • Please inform organisers of any allergies
  • Meals prepared with love by temple volunteers
