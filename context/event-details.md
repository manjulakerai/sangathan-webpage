# Event Details — 25th Anniversary

## Organisation
- **Name:** SSMB Sydney (Shree Swaminarayan Mandir Bhuj, Sydney)
- **Registered as:** Shree Swaminarayan Temple Sydney Inc.
- **Established:** 26 June 2001
- **Community:** Kutchi Haribhakto in Sydney

## Event
- **Theme:** Sangathan Parva | A Celebration of Unity
- **Dates:** 4 July – 12 July 2026
- **Milestone:** 25 Years of Establishment
- **Purpose:** Strengthen bonds among haribhakto across Sydney, promote unity, and inspire increased bhajan bhakti

## History
- **December 2000:** Journey began with blessings of Mahant Sadguru Shree Hariswarupdasji Swami to establish a spiritual home for Kutchi Haribhakto in Sydney
- **31 May 2001:** Upmahant Sadguru Shree Dharmanandandasji Swami penned a heartfelt letter blessing the vision of building a Mandir for Ishtadev Shree Swaminarayan Bhagwan in Sydney. The letter emphasised mutual affection, brotherhood, and **sangathan** (unity) — the origin of the event's name.
- **26 June 2001:** Official registration of Shree Swaminarayan Temple Sydney Inc.
- Year-long celebration leading up to the grand event in July 2026

## Key Figures
- Aksharnivasi Mahant Sadguru Shree Hariswarupdasji Swami
- Mahant Sadguru Shree Dharmandandasji Swami

## Word Origin
- **Sangathan** [san-ga-ṭana] — *noun* — The quality of being united into one
